Tsingubo tsiomwisomero School clothes Les vêtements d'école

Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi

Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter

Catherine Were

Oluwanga

Level 1

The audio for this story is currently not available.

Liriinda lino ne liraambi.

This dress is long.

Cette robe est longue.

Omuchosi kuno nomukhoongo.

This jersey is big.

Ce pull est grand.

Iloloti ino neikhoongo.

This bag is big.

Ce sac est grand.

Omukoye kwomushibuno…

This belt is…

Cette ceinture est…

Ikofia ino neinditi.

This hat is small.

Ce chapeau est petit.

Tsisokisi netsinyimbikiti.

The socks are short.

Les chaussettes sont courtes.

Halali efilaro fino nefiyiakha.

But these shoes are new.

Mais ces souliers sont neufs.

.... fiyela obulayi mufilenje.

… they fit well.

… ils font bien.

Written by: Clare Verbeek, Thembani Dladla, Zanele Buthelezi
Illustrated by: Mlungisi Dlamini, Ingrid Schechter
Translated by: Catherine Were
Language: Oluwanga
Level: Level 1
